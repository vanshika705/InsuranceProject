Project Details

In this project python, Flask app, various Machine Learing algorithms such as Linear Regression, Decision Tree Regression and Random Forest Regression are used. Along with these, Data Integrity and Web development is also played important role. When user enters the input like customer's age, number of children a customer have, customer is a smoker or not,in which region of country customer lives, customer's gender, customer's BMI, customer's health criteria then premium charges of customer will be printed on the user's screen.
